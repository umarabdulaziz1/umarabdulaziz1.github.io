Ingredients

  • 800g chicken
  • 1/2-2 coconut milk
  • 180g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

    Cooking steps

  1. Saute the curry paste in hot oil until fragant
  2. Add in chicken
  3. Continue to saute until chicken release oil
  4. add in
    • Tamarind Paste
    • curry leaves
    • Potato
    • onion
    • Sugar
    • Salt
  5. add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to seperate
